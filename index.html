About

Sentinel ID was built out of frustration. Legacy MFA is broken — SMS codes get intercepted, authenticator apps get lost, and hardware tokens get left on desks.

We rebuilt multi-factor authentication from scratch. Our protocol combines device-bound cryptographic keys, behavioral biometrics, and real-time threat signals into a single seamless step.

No codes. No popups. No friction. Just zero-trust verification that works the way security should — invisibly, instantly, and everywhere.
